Medical Adult Day Care

At Emmanuel Care, our Medical Adult Day Care program is thoughtfully designed to support seniors and adults with health challenges who wish to maintain their independence while receiving essential medical and personal care services. Our program offers a safe, welcoming environment where participants can enjoy daily socialization, therapeutic activities, and professional health services — all tailored to meet their individual needs.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between independent living and full-time nursing care. We provide participants with access to a variety of supportive services throughout the day, allowing them to return home to their families each evening. By combining clinical support with engaging activities, we help promote emotional well-being, physical health, and a sense of community, giving families peace of mind and participants a vibrant place to thrive.

Expert Skilled Nursing Services

At Emmanuel Care, our Medical Adult Day Care services are customized to support a wide range of health and daily living needs, including:

Skilled Nursing Care

Our licensed nurses provide ongoing medical monitoring, medication management, chronic condition management, wound care, and support for health concerns such as diabetes, hypertension, and post-surgical recovery.

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

We offer professional physical therapy designed to relieve pain, restore mobility, strengthen muscles, prevent falls, and support recovery from injuries, surgeries, or strokes.

Personal Care Assistance

Compassionate assistance with activities of daily living (ADLs), including help with bathing, grooming, toileting, dressing, and mobility support, all while preserving dignity and independence.

Nutritious Meals and Special Diets

Freshly prepared meals are provided daily, with menus developed under the supervision of a licensed dietitian. Special dietary accommodations are available to meet participants’ unique medical or cultural needs.

Social Services and Counseling

Our professional social workers assist participants and families with accessing community resources, counseling services, and help with financial aid or benefits navigation.

Therapeutic and Recreational Activities

A variety of programs including cognitive games, art and music therapy, exercise classes, and educational workshops promote mental engagement, physical activity, and emotional wellness.

Transportation Services

Safe, reliable, wheelchair-accessible transportation is available to and from the center, including rides to medical appointments, ensuring participants' mobility and continuity of care.

Specialized Memory Care Activities

Tailored activities and therapies are available for participants living with Alzheimer’s disease or other forms of dementia, helping maintain cognitive function and providing a sense of comfort and engagement.

Who Can Benefit from Medical Day Care?

Our program is ideal for individuals who require daily medical monitoring, rehabilitation services, personal care assistance, or social engagement but prefer to live at home with their families. Participants may include:

Adults recovering from surgery, injury, or stroke

Seniors managing chronic conditions such as heart disease, diabetes, or arthritis

Individuals living with cognitive impairments such as Alzheimer's disease or dementia

Adults who would benefit from therapeutic activities and structured support in a social environment

Insurance and Coverage

Emmanuel Care’s Medical Adult Day Care services are covered by Medicare, Medicaid, and most major private insurance plans. Our team can assist you in navigating insurance options to ensure you or your loved one receives the services needed without unnecessary financial burden.
