Personalized Social Services

Our social work services are tailored to meet the specific needs of each participant, ensuring comprehensive support across multiple areas. From problem-solving to counseling, financial assistance, and more, we offer a wide range of services designed to promote independence and stability.

Problem-Solving & Planning

Assisting participants and families in identifying challenges and formulating plans to address them, utilizing community resources to maintain independence.

Counseling Services

Providing emotional support and counseling for individuals, groups, and families as needed.

Financial Assistance

Helping eligible participants access government benefits such as welfare, food stamps, SSI, Medicaid, and more.

Intake Interviews & Home Assessments

Conducting thorough assessments to evaluate financial, medical, social, and psychological aspects of participants' lives.

Record Keeping

Maintaining confidential records of participants' social and emotional statuses, care plans, and progress.

In-Service Training

Collaborating with staff to ensure quality care and offering educational programs to families and community organizations.

Community Resource Referrals

Connecting participants with external community resources to expand the scope of their care.
