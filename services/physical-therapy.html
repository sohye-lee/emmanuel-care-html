Comprehensive Physical Therapy Services

At Emmanuel Care, our physical therapy (PT) services are designed to help seniors improve their physical function, reduce pain, and enhance their overall quality of life. Our therapy sessions focus on preserving, restoring, and improving movement abilities that may be affected by disease, injury, or disability. With a range of therapeutic exercises, physical modalities, and personalized training, our goal is to help participants live more independently and comfortably.

Physical Therapy Treatment Types Include

Posture Correction

Fall Prevention Training

Thermal Therapy

Electrical Stimulation Therapy

Ultrasound

Paraffin Wax

Infrared Therapy

Manual Therapy

Muscle Strengthening

Endurance Training

Cardiovascular Strengthening

Physical therapy at Emmanuel Care is covered by Medicare and Medicaid, with Medicare recipients eligible for 25-30 treatments annually based on individual medical needs.
