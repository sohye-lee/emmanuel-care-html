Comprehensive Nursing Services

At Emmanuel Care, our registered nurses are committed to providing exceptional health-related services for seniors with chronic care conditions. Our experienced team is trained to manage various health challenges in a safe and supportive environment, ensuring that each individual’s needs are met with respect and compassion. We specialize in caring for participants with conditions such as advanced arthritis, asthma, Alzheimer’s disease, COPD, diabetes, multiple sclerosis, multi-infarct dementia, Parkinson’s disease, and stroke, among others.

Personalized and Specialized Care

Our staff is highly skilled in managing essential daily care needs such as toileting, eating, and bathing, including safe transfers. Each center is equipped with walk-in showers to ensure convenience and safety during personal care. We also provide private care for participants with specialized needs like tube feeding, ensuring the utmost dignity and privacy in our nurse’s clinic.

Bathing & Grooming

Safe and respectful assistance with personal hygiene, including transfers.

Specialized Needs

Support for tube feeding and other unique care requirements in a private setting.

Chronic Condition Management

Customized care plans for participants managing multiple chronic conditions.

Expert Skilled Nursing Services

Our skilled nursing services are designed to address a wide range of medical needs. Whether it’s post-operative care, medication management, or wound care, our registered nurses are equipped to provide advanced medical support tailored to each participant's unique situation. We also offer specialized services for seniors with Alzheimer’s and dementia, ensuring they receive the best care possible.
