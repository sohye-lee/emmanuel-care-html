A Message from Our Founder

Welcome to Emmanuel Care, where we bring over 30 years of nursing experience from hospitals across the United States to the heart of our operations. Since founding our Home Health service in 2011, we have been dedicated to providing exemplary care to those who need it most. In 2021, we expanded our passion for care by opening our Adult Day Care center.

Now in our third year, we are proud to operate in one of the finest facilities in the region, specifically designed to nurture both the physical and mental well-being of our clients. At Emmanuel Care, we focus on creating a supportive and enriching environment that allows every individual to thrive. Join us in our commitment to exceptional care and community support.

Spacious & Modern Facility

Emmanuel Care boasts an impressive 20,000 square feet of space, making us the largest adult day care center in the area. Our facility is thoughtfully designed and beautifully equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable, pleasant, and stimulating environment for all who visit. From spacious common areas to specialized activity rooms, we have created a setting that fosters community engagement, relaxation, and well-being for seniors.

Reliable & Convenient Transportation

We understand that reliable transportation is crucial for seniors and their families. That’s why Emmanuel Care offers a comprehensive transportation service that is both safe and comfortable. We provide door-to-door transportation, including pick-ups from home to the center and return trips at the end of the day. We also offer transportation to and from medical appointments, ensuring that seniors can access the care they need without any added stress. Our vehicles are regularly maintained and our drivers are trained to prioritize the safety and comfort of each passenger.

Engaging & Supportive Environment

At Emmanuel Care, we are committed to providing not just a safe space, but a truly loving and supportive environment where seniors can thrive. Our programs are carefully designed to engage participants on a physical, emotional, and cognitive level, ensuring that each individual receives the holistic care and attention they deserve. Whether through social activities, personalized care, or therapeutic services, we create an atmosphere that fosters connection, independence, and joy—ensuring that your loved ones feel valued and engaged in a community that truly cares for them.
