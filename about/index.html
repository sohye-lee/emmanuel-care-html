Our History

Emmanuel Care was founded out of a genuine need for quality senior care, sparked by the experience of our founder, a dedicated Registered Nurse (RN). After facing the challenges of finding reliable help for her own loved one, she recognized the importance of compassionate, personalized care for seniors. This journey led to the establishment of Emmanuel Home Health Services, a locally owned and operated center committed to providing exceptional care.

With deep roots in our community, we pride ourselves on offering top-notch services at an affordable price. Our local ownership allows us to maintain minimal operating expenses, ensuring that our residents receive the highest level of care without the inflated costs often associated with franchised agencies. At Emmanuel Care, we combine professional expertise with a heartfelt understanding of the unique needs of seniors, creating a nurturing environment where they can thrive.
